The Voice & The Pen

I’m Sadia Shahzad, a Mass Communication student at a federal university, specializing in Broadcast Media, Film, and Theatre. Writing is my passion — especially crime thrillers, suspense, murder mysteries, and social commentaries.

For me, media and literature are powerful vessels to bring awareness and trigger positive change in society, using characters to reflect our deepest realities.

Literary Collections

I’ve written an afsana, Intezaar, which delves deep into the concepts of sacrifice and consequence, an article, Azadi aik Naymat, exploring the true value and gift of Pakistan, and an Eid novelette, Eid e Vasl, focusing on family bonds and emotional ties.

Current Works

I’m currently working on my novel, Aas, which is a crime thriller that explores societal injustice and power dynamics, along with another novelette that focuses on justice, media ethics, and pressing social issues.

Inspirations

Reading and reflecting on the different perspectives of life is what inspires my work. For me, stories are not just a form of expression, but a way to hold up a mirror to the world.

I hope this digital library serves as a cozy sanctuary for you. Feel free to explore my portfolios, download articles, read reviews, or send a direct message.
